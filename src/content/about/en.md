I'm Hamza, and I have always believed that history is best told through the eyes of those who love it. With a deep academic background in linguistics and years of experience traversing the historic sites of Algeria, I treat every tour as a narrative. For me, guiding isn't just about dates and names; it's about connecting people to the spirit of a place.

---

Being recognized in international travel circles is a pride I carry into every walk. I strive to provide a bridge between Tlemcen's glorious past and its vibrant present. When I'm not guiding guests through the Great Mosque or the ruins of Mansourah, you'll likely find me managing a local literary café or overseeing the production of traditional olive oil — always looking for ways to celebrate and preserve our local traditions.

---

I am an enthusiast of the small details — the perfect light for a landscape photo, the nuances of regional dialects, and the hospitality that defines Algerian life. My goal is to ensure that when you leave, you don't just have photos; you have a genuine understanding of what makes this corner of the world so extraordinary.
