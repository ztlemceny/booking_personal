The true soul of Tlemcen lives in the medina's narrow alleys. We'll wander through the bustling souks, visit ancestral fondouks, and stop at communal ovens where bread still bakes as it did in ancestors' times.

A total sensory experience: spices, textiles, craftsmen at work, and authentic Algerian hospitality. The communal bread oven is one of Hamza's favorite stops — these neighborhood hubs where news and warmth are exchanged, where the smell of freshly baked bread is the most sincere welcome.

This tour reveals the small details of daily life that most travelers never see, but which give this culture its profound beauty.
